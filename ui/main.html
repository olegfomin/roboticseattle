Hello! My name is S.P.A.R. That stands for the Self Propelled Autonomous Robot. I am a machine that can be controlled over the internet right from your browser! The 4G network connection allows me to utilize the power of the Amazon cloud anywhere I go. I am build to drive over the rough terrains of the real world outside. I am a unique prototype that can be used for various applications in the future.

How it all started ...

Many real world tasks can be automated thus making your world a better place. Right now this process takes place inside the factory buildings while outdoors are mostly left for the people to explore and take care of. The S.P.A.R. project was originally intended to demonstrate that the smart moving machines can be very useful in some routine tasks outside. The growing 4G networks provide the fast and reliable connection to a computer cloud therefore allowing the robots to receive that extra bit of brain power they were lacking before. In other words we as humans have reached the tipping point in robotic evolution and the S.P.A.R. is going to prove it! The devices like this will turn the evolution into the long awaited revolution. The ultimate goal of the S.P.A.R. and its little brother was to become an automated gold searching machine that utilizes a metal detector attached as a tool.


S.P.A.R. is unique ...

S.P.A.R. is one of the very first machines that combines the following features and ideas into one solution:

  • A life operator can control S.P.A.R. over the Internet
  • The Artificial Intelligence decisions can be made by a 'cloud'
  • Designed to work outdoors (see chassis specification)
  • Able to listen and see (camera and microphone)
  • Equipped with four ultrasonic sonars (Parallax sonar)
  • Able speak English and sing two songs
  • Battery replacement mechanism (See how simple & reliable)
  • Able to express facial emotions
  • Pan/Tilt for camera and microphone (here is how)
  • Self calibrating motors (based on a hall effect sensor)

Automated battery exchange system ...

The batteries are everywhere. Having to need the philips screwdriver to exchange the disposable batteries for electricity powered equipment is rather preposterous. Putting the electronic device into the docking station or equivalent for a prolong period of time is not ideal either. What should have been done long ago is a battery exchange mechanism that would replace a depleted battery with a newly charged one in a matter of seconds. It would have been an ideal solution if the process is completely automated not requiring any human involvement. For the robots like S.P.A.R. I believe it is an absolutely essential high priority task that would allow the robot to fulfil its duties day and night without unnecessary interruptions ...

Moving target ...

People usually spend 30% of their time foraging for food and about 70% for entertainment. Have you ever been to a shooting range? Let's admit it we, especially males, like to hit a target with a projectile. However it gets boring after a while if the target is static and does not move. I really hope that my project to create an armored and re-enforced robot that can move around will take this boredom away. Imagine you can put a cheap dinosaur model on top and start firing away at it from the distance while the robot makes evasive moves and slowly approaching you. It can be quite fun I believe. Would you think it is not too difficult to implement?

Search for gold and spiders ...

Ok, it was my original idea to supply a robot with a metal detector and deploy this machine into the depth of the Washington or Oregon States in search for gold. However the creeks turned out to be quite a challenging terrain for the robot to navigate through. Moving a little bit south may make change your priorities from gold searching to evading venomous things for both you and your kids. That's where I started thinking for scanning the area in search for sources of danger. Let's admit it, the Raspberry Pi is not qualified much for the computer vision (CV) tasks however the 4G networks are wide spread in the developed world and a 'cloud' has enough power to discern a spider of a snake on a picture.

Tractor plows the soil ...

I was fascinated about the sheer power and look of tractors from my early childhood. I am a firm believer that as soon as everyone gets a healthy layer of fat under their skin and all of us got the fresh water to drink and a decent shelter to reside under with our kids then the most acute conflicts in our lives will vanish without trace. Can a robotic tractor help? The tractor are usually slow moving vehicles and they are working on the wide open spaces and therefore they can be autonomous and/or remotely controlled/supervised over the 4G networks, aren't they? Put a robot on a mini tractor and supply that machine with a couple of strong hands and you shall have an ultimate farm helper that would make an entire planet well fed and full of all necessary items.

Software development in general ...

The main question any Software Development company should answer is how to ensure the quality of the software you produce and at the same time how to support the creativity of Engineers/Developers. Honestly while you are in the creative state then the last thing you are thinking of is the coding standards and corporate guidelines. My personal answer to this question is going to be a two-face check-in and a little bit more:

  • DO: Creativity&Quality with two-face check-in
  • DO: Small motivated groups above all
    • The development teams must stay small and we must be motivated at any cost ...
  • DO: Early integration between teams!
    • Collaboration between teams is essential! The frequent fun company-wide events are paramount plus the equal number of female and male participants in any given team ...
  • DO: Make it Simple & Reliable!
  • STOP: Too many sleepy meetings
    • Please refer to the 'Dilbert cartoons about meetings'. They are very true ...
  • STOP: Disregarding your team moral impact
    • Engineers are highly capable people and they are usually equipped with the good long term memory. They are not going to forget if their management acts like: "They are getting paid then who cares what they feel ...". If you must deliver undesirable news to the team then you gotta take time to explain yourself!
  • STOP: Being Penny Wise and the Pound Foolish
  • STOP: Being addicted to the drama
    • I fully agree to the 'BLACK EYED PEAS' band words: "I think the whole world addicted to the drama. Only attracted to things that'll bring you trauma"
